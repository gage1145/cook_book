Banana Bread - Shug

This will make anyone's day better.

Ingredients

  • 3 medium overripe bananas
  • 2 Large eggs
  • 1 cup buttermilk
  • 1 tsp vanilla
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 2 1/2 cups flour
  • 1 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1 tsp salt
